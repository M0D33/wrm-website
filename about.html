Our Story

Built in Saudi Arabia, for Saudi Arabia

White Rocks Mining Company was established in 2022 in Dammam, Saudi Arabia, with the ambition to become the kingdom's leading supplier of premium industrial silica minerals. From our earliest days, we invested in the right infrastructure, people, and processes to deliver on that promise.

By 2023, we had launched operations in Tayma, secured our first major supply contracts, achieved ISO certification, and established our in-house logistics management system. In 2024, we expanded further with the opening of our SPARK facility, broadening our reach and production capacity across the Kingdom.

Today, WRM operates from three strategic locations across Saudi Arabia.
