Silica Flour

Silica Flour is ultra-fine milled silica produced through precision ball-milling and air classification, delivering tight particle size control down to sub-50 micron ranges. Its high chemical purity and consistent fineness make it indispensable in oil-well cementing, ceramics, paints, coatings, rubber compounding, and specialty chemical manufacturing.

Industrial Mineral · Product 03

Frac Sand

WRM Frac Sand is a high-purity, high-strength proppant engineered for hydraulic fracturing operations. Its high roundness and sphericity prop open fractures in reservoir rock, maximising hydrocarbon flow. All physical parameters are tested to API/ISO 13503-2 standards covering crush resistance, roundness, sphericity, turbidity, and acid solubility.
